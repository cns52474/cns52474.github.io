Welcome to My Page!

Hello! I am Caroline Nicole Sweat, a freshman here at UGA! I grew up in Wrightsville, Georgia and attended Brentwood School from Kindergarten until twelfth grade. At UGA, my biggest class is 300 people. In high school, my entire graduating class included only 25 people. It has been difficult to transition from a small town like Wrightsville to Athens. I have always been extremely close with my family, so being away from them has been a challenge. Luckily, I got plugged into some amazing organizations and immediately felt at home here in Athens.

I am actively involved in Cru, a campus ministry here at UGA. I participate in retreats, socials, and sing with the praise brand regularly. I am also a member of the sorority, Delta Delta Delta. I joined this sorority last semester and it has been one of the greatest decisions I have ever made for myself. Next year, I will have the opportunity to live in the sorority house with my five best friends: Joy, Abby, Gabby, Lily, and Emma.
